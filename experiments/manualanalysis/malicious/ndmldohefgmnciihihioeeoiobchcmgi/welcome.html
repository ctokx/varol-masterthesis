Shortcut not working?
The shortcut keys may not work if the key combination is already being used by another extension.
To re-allocate your shortcut combination keys, click here.

  1. Open Chrome Shortcuts Manager- chrome://extensions/shortcuts
  2. Find & scroll to "Flow: ChatGPT Sidebar"
  3. Click on the edit button to change the shortcut key for opening/activating the side panel:
  4. Click on the input area & press Alt + C to re-assign & save the shortcut key combination:
